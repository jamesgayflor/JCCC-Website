About Us

The John Christian International School System (JCISS) for short is a private Christian Institution. It was established Monday September 2nd, 2010. The John Christian International School System is one of the many prestigious and reputable Institutions in post-war Liberia. In its quest to improve the educational system in the country, the John Christian International School system has decided Building good people for a better nation through human resource development in the mist of vices that tend to demoralize and devalue academic standard and spiritual excellence in Liberia...
